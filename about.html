About Vatant

Maps have always been the source of my love for history. As a politics and history buff, I have spent hours looking at maps and wondering how differently the world could have turned out in the past or what changes could happen in the future. This project of collecting and inferring data from around the globe began in the summer of 2023 as a way to get people thinking beyond borders drawn a century ago and imagine something beyond them.


If you notice any errors or have insight on any part of the world, contact us at info@vatant.com.
